Robber and Cop

  • Originality: You play both roles of a heist! First as the robber escaping police, then as the police officer chasing following your own trail. It requires some fast reflexes and memorization to play!
  • Technical Merit: The game randomly decides where each obstacle will go, and also remembers the locations of each coin that is dropped. These locations are saved and accessed in arrays. The scrolling background is also just an illusion. It's just a gif of the road moving while your car is stationary.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned a couple of minor things in this project. How to use arrays, create custom bounds, and how to manage groups of objects. -The easiest part was randomly spawning cones and making sure the coins spawned correctly. -The hardest part was getting the collision to work between the cones and the car, though that didn't take too long to adjust.
  • Prototype Assets: All art assets are taken from other sources.
    • Robber's car: Red Car by lawrence_laz
    • Police car: Police Cars by clayster2012
    • Traffic cone: Traffic cone on pixelartmaker.com
    • Money: Rotating coin by Puddin
    • Road texture: City Slum Tileset Mock-Up by u/zealotlee
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Prototype closely resembles other top down driving games like Spy Hunter and GTA. Uses code from the "Sprite Pool" and "Breakout" Phaser examples.
